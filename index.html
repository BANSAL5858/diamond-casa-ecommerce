DIAMOND CASA PROMISE

What are lab grown diamonds?

+

Lab-grown diamonds are diamonds created in a laboratory setting using advanced technological processes that mimic the natural diamond-growing environment. They have the same chemical composition, physical properties and optical characteristics as natural diamonds but are ethically and sustainably produced.

How lab grown diamonds are made?

+

Lab-grown diamonds are created using two primary methods: High Pressure High Temperature (HPHT) and Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD). In HPHT, carbon is subjected to high pressure and temperature, mimicking natural diamond formation. CVD involves introducing a carbon-containing gas into a chamber, where it breaks down and deposits diamond material onto a substrate.

What is the price and budget of lab-grown diamonds compared to natural diamonds?

+

The price of lab-grown diamonds is generally lower compared to natural diamonds of similar quality. Lab-grown diamonds offer a more affordable option due to their controlled production process and avoidance of mining expenses.

Are lab diamonds real?

+

Yes, lab diamonds are identical to earth mined diamonds. There is no difference between the two except to their origin. They are certified & graded by the same international agencies that certify Mined diamonds (IGI or GIA).

How can I make out difference between a lab diamond and an earth mined diamond?

+

You cannot. 8 billion people in the world, NO ONE, not even superman can make out difference between lab and earth diamonds. Only machine testing can point that out. They are chemically & optically identical.

Do you give a certificate of authentication?

+

Yes, all solitaire diamonds above 0.50 carats come with a unique IGI certificate. The certificate number is in-scripted on the diamond girdle with a laser.
